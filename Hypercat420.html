Hypercat420 is normally a YouTube Content creator, but as he goes inside the world of Low-Effort-Music, his music can sometimes sound very great. Known for his playful approach to music-making, Hypercat420's unique style incorporates elements of electronic music.

Hypercat420 has been carving out a niche for himself in the music industry with his unapologetically fun and laid-back approach.  He does put his full effort on some songs. Most notably for his EP's.

Influenced by artists such as Justice, Yuksek, Home, NtGm 889, Lemon Demon, and many more artists. Hypercat420 brings his own unique spin to the genre, infusing it with his signature blend of humor and creativity.

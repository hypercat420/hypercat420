About HC402 RECORDS

HC402 RECORDS is an independent record label with a passion for nurturing talent and promoting diverse musical expressions. Founded by Hypercat420, a multi-talented artist and YouTube content creator, our mission is to provide a platform for emerging artists to showcase their creativity and connect with a global audience.

At HC402 RECORDS, we believe in the power of music to transcend boundaries. We welcome artists from all corners of the world, regardless of their genre or style. Whether you're a singer-songwriter, a hip-hop producer, an electronic music enthusiast, or an experimental sound creator, we are dedicated to helping you reach your full potential.

Our commitment to artists extends beyond recording and distribution. We offer a range of services, including professional audio production, music promotion, and artist development. We are here to support you every step of the way, from refining your sound to building your fan base.

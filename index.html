About HC402 RECORDS

HC402 RECORDS was founded by Hypercat420, a multi-talented artist and YouTube content creator.

Hypercat420, known for his playful approach to music-making, incorporates elements of electronic music into his work. His unapologetically fun and laid-back style has carved out a niche in the music industry. His tracks are filled with catchy hooks and infectious beats that are impossible to resist.

Influenced by artists like Justice, Hypercat420 brings his unique spin to the genre, infusing it with his signature blend of humor and creativity.
